Conclave is a fast, no-fuss video meeting app. Start a room in one tap, share the link, and anyone can join from their phone or a browser - no account required.

MEET IN SECONDS
• One tap to start a meeting as a guest, or sign in with Google
• Join with a room code or an invite link
• Locked rooms with a waiting room the host controls
• Crisp, dark, distraction-free meeting screen

EVERYTHING A CALL NEEDS
• Reliable audio and video with quick mute and camera controls
• Screen sharing from your phone
• Live transcription with captions for the whole room
• Emoji reactions and raise-hand
• Picture-in-picture and background calling, so the call continues while you multitask

CHAT THAT FEELS RIGHT
• Fast in-meeting chat with replies and message actions
• GIF search built in
• Private messages when you need a side conversation

PLAY TOGETHER
Turn any meeting into game night. The built-in catalog runs right on the meeting stage:
• Trivia, Bluff, Would You Rather, Most Likely To, Reaction
• Imposter - spot the faker in the room
• Wordle - same word, solo boards, fastest solve wins
• Chess - duel a friend, play in teams, or take on the computer

BUILT FOR YOUR TEAM AND YOUR FRIENDS
Conclave works seamlessly with the Conclave web app, so people on laptops and phones share the same rooms, chat, and games.
